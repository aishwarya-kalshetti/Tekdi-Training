About

The Student Registration Portal is a user-friendly platform designed to simplify the process of student enrollment. It allows students to register for courses, update personal details, and access important academic information from one centralized location. With its intuitive interface and organized layout, the portal ensures that students can complete their registrations quickly and accurately, saving time for both students and administrators.
